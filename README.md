# Ecommerce-Sales-Dashboard-Tableau

An interactive Tableau dashboard built to analyze e-commerce sales, customer behavior, product performance, and business insights. This project demonstrates dashboard design, KPI development, calculated fields, interactive filters, and data storytelling using Tableau.

---

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales performance
- Track key business KPIs
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare regional profitability
- Understand customer purchasing patterns
- Enable interactive business analysis through filters

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- 💰 Total Sales
- 📈 Total Profit
- 📦 Total Orders
- 👥 Total Customers
- 🛒 Average Order Value (AOV)
- 📊 Profit Margin (%)

---

## Dashboard Features

### 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
Visualizes monthly sales performance to identify seasonal trends.

### 🛍️ Sales by Category
Compares total sales across different product categories.

### 🌍 Profit by Region
Highlights the most profitable regions.

### 🏆 Top 10 Products by Sales
Identifies the highest revenue-generating products.

### 💳 Payment Method Distribution
Shows customer payment preferences.

### 📉 Sales vs Profit Analysis
Analyzes the relationship between sales and profit using a scatter plot.

### 🎛️ Interactive Filters
- Region
- Category
- Payment Method
- Month
